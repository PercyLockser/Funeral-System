FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


    What is the purpose of a funeral service?
    A funeral service serves as a way to honor and celebrate the life 
    of the deceased, providing an opportunity for family and friends 
    to come together to mourn, share memories, and find closure.

    What types of funeral services are available?
    Funeral services can vary widely, from traditional religious 
    ceremonies to more personalized celebrations of life. Options 
    include visitations, viewings, funerals, memorial services, and 
    graveside services.

    What should I include in the obituary?
    An obituary typically includes biographical information about 
    the deceased, details about the funeral service, surviving family 
    members, and any special accomplishments or interests. Our team 
    can assist you in crafting a fitting tribute.

    What are the options for burial or cremation?
    Burial and cremation are the two most common options for disposition 
    of the deceased. We can help you explore the various options available, 
    including traditional burials, cremation with or without a funeral 
    service, and alternative forms of disposition such as green burials 
    or scatterings.

    How far in advance should I plan a funeral service?
    It's never too early to start planning a funeral service, but in 
    general,it's best to begin the planning process as soon as possible 
    after the death of a loved one. Our staff is available to assist 
    you at any stage of the planning process.

    What is the cost of a funeral service?
    The cost of a funeral service can vary depending on factors such as 
    the type of service, choice of products, and additional services 
    requested. Our staff can provide a detailed breakdown of costs and 
    assist you in making decisions that fit your budget.

    How do I choose the right type of service for my loved one?
    Consider your loved one's wishes, religious or cultural traditions, 
    and personal preferences when selecting the type of service. Our 
    experienced staff can also provide guidance and support in making 
    this decision.
